The Best Anime Series According to Shey

My top 3 Anime of all time.

One Piece

This is one of my current ongoing series this 2024 and I really liked it because it has different kinds of adventures and it shows true meaning of friendship through Straw Hat Pirates. I really admire each characters and their relationship. I love Chopper and Luffy!

Jujutsu Kaisen

This was the first anime series I watched and finished on Netflix. I also love this anime because of its unique story line, the powers and characters are superb. I already finished reading its Manga and honestly, I encountered different kinds of conflicts but it's really worth it. It is still ongoing though and I am still looking forward to the story.

Apothecary Diaries

This is also one of my ongoing anime on Netflix. I love the relationship that Jinshi and Maomao have. I am always rooting on every episode and the story itself is really addicting. I can't wait to read its Manga.
